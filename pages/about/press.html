Press Coverage

KAT masters are recognized experts on martial arts and we have been fortunate enough to have many amazing students throughout the years. Here is a sampling of some of the news coverage our school and students have received. Since its founding in the fall of 2012, there are many articles about KAT students and instructors at coloradomanews.com.
